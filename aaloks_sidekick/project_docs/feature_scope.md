# 🎯 Feature Scope Map

## 🟩 In Scope (Phase 1A)
* 8 Aggregated KPI metrics widgets.
* Dynamic filter bar with Plants, Suppliers, and entries size dropdowns.
* Context drawer displaying supplier risk indicators, lead times, safety stocks, and recent email logs.
* Custom HSL scrollbar and outfit typography dark theme variables.

## 🟥 Out of Scope (Phase 1B+)
* Email automated drafts.
* AI live chatbot assistants.
* ERP/SAP reschedule triggers.
